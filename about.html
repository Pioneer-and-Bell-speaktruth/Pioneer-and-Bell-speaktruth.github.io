About the Pioneer & Bell church of Christ

Who We Are

We exist for one purpose: to spread the Gospel of Christ to every soul we can reach (Matt. 28:18–20). We seek to be nothing more — and nothing less — than the Christians you read about in the New Testament. No creeds. No councils. No human doctrines. Just the Bible, the Gospel, and a desire to follow Jesus exactly as He taught.

What This Website Is For

This site serves as a resource for our congregation and our community. Here you will find articles, sermon outlines, announcements, and tools to help you grow in your walk with Christ.
