A MATTER OF HEAVEN AND HELL

In a January 26, 2008 interview of Mark Henderson by Carla Hinton of the "Daily Oklahoman", Mark told about the decision of the Quail Springs Church of Christ to use instruments of music in their worship. Our concern should be: "What does the Scripture Say?" (Rom. 3:3) It is a matter of Heaven and Hell.

If Ephesians 5:19 and Colossians 3:16 authorized instrumental music in praise of God, then each individual in the congregation would have to play an instrument! God simply authorizes us to sing. Why would we want to add to God's will?

God loves us more than we can understand. He gave His Son to die for our sins. God is worthy of our worship, as revealed in the New Testament. We should not seek to be entertained. We should want to worship God in spirit and truth (John 4:2). When our worship pleases God, we glorify Him. God wants to bless us, and He will when we conform our lives to His great will. "Your are worth, O Lord, to receive glory and honor and power: for You created all things, and by Your will they exist and were created" (Revelation 4:11).

Timothy Sparks

Leedey, OK
