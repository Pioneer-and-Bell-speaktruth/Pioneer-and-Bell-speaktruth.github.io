WHAT IS THE CHURCH?

(Matthew 16:13-19)

I. INTRODUCTION:

Who determines what IS the church? Man? Or God?

If man, then ANY definition will do.

BUT, If God, then only HIS definition will do.

“Church” = Ek Lesia [EKKLESIA] = The “called out ones”

Church in N.T. = Universal All Christians (Matthew 16:18)

Local One Congregation (1 Corinthians 1:2)

Assembly (1 Corinthians 14:28,34)

Church of Christ (Romans 16:16)

---One body (Ephesians 4:4)

---Body is the saved (Acts 2:41,47) (Ephesians 5:23)

THEREFORE (in N.T.) = Church = Saved & Saved = Church
