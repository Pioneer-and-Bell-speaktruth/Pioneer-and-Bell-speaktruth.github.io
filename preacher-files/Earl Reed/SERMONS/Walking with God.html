WALKING WITH GOD
Micah 6:8 (KJV)

He hath shewed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require

of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God?

Enoch walked with God (Genesis 5:24)

Noah walked with God (Genesis 6:9)

The principles* in this verse have been required in every dispensation/covenant with God.

The LORD requires….

Do justly  (do right)
     John 8:28   Jesus
    John 7:21-24
    Matthew 7:12

Love mercy    (compassion)
     Matthew 9:35-36    Jesus
     1 Peter 3:8ff

Walk humbly with thy God
     Matthew 18:1-6
     Matthew 23:11-12
     James 4:1-12
     1 Peter 5:1-11

1 John 1:5-10

1 John 2:6

*Principle: 1a: a comprehensive and fundamental law, doctrine, or assumption

b: a rule or code of conduct
