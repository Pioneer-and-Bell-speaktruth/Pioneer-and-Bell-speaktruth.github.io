III. In Conversion

A. By Word of God

1. The three thousand, “When they heard this” Acts 2:37-38

2. The five thousand, “Many heard the word” (Acts 4:4)  Howbeit many of them which heard the word believed; and the number of the men was about five thousand.

3. The Samaritans, “They heard Phillip preaching” (Acts 8:12)  But when they believed Philip preaching the things concerning the kingdom of God, and the name of Jesus Christ, they were baptized, both men and women.

4. The Eunuch, “He preached unto him Jesus” Acts 8:35-38

5. Saul of Tarsus, “Shall be told thee what to do” (Acts 9:6)  And he trembling and astonished said, Lord, what wilt thou have me to do? And the Lord said unto him, Arise, and go into the city, and it shall be told thee what thou must do. 1 2

6. Cornelius, “Words whereby.....saved” (Acts 11:12)  And the Spirit bade me go with them, nothing doubting. Moreover these six brethren accompanied me, and we entered into the man's house: And he shewed us how he had seen an angel in his house, which stood and said unto him, Send men to Joppa, and call for Simon, whose surname is Peter; Who shall tell thee words, whereby thou and all thy house shall be saved.

7. Lydia, “spake – heard” (Acts 16:14)  And a certain woman named Lydia, a seller of purple, of the city of Thyatira, which worshipped God, heard us: whose heart the Lord opened, that she attended unto the things which were spoken of Paul.

8. Jailer, “Spake unto him the word” Acts 16:32

9. The Corinthians, “Hearing – believed – baptized” Acts 18:8

10. Bereans, “Received word.....believed” Acts 17:11-12

11. The Twelve, “When they heard this.....baptized” Acts 19:5
