III. Is it for Today?

A. Summary of 1st Century Anointing

1. Jesus was ‘anointed’ to be king

2. The Apostles were ‘anointed’ to be Jesus’ representatives and “witnesses” of His resurrection. John 15:26-27; Acts 1:16-21; 23:11

3. Some Christians were ‘anointed’ when the Apostles laid hands upon them to receive miraculous gifts from the Holy Spirit Acts 8:9-19; 19:1-6

4. Miracles (a result of ‘anointing’) were to “confirm the word” being preached Mark 16:14-20; Hebrews 2:1-4

B. Why ‘anointing’ is not commanded or needed today

1. In the 1st Century, the Gospel (New Testament) was:

a. revealed and understandable

b. confirmed with miracles from the Holy Spirit

c. completed in the 1st Century.

C. There is no NEW revelation today, therefore no need for miraculous ‘anointing”
