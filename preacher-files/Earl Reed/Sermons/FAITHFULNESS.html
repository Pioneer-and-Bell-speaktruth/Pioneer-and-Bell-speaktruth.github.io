Faithfulness

EDR 1988 D EFINITION Faith = allegiance to duty or a person. Fidelity to one’s promises, complete trust, without doubt or question. Faithful = Full of faith. Steadfast in affection or allegiance; LOYAL, Firm adherence to promises or in observance of duty. A firm resistance to any temptation to desert or betray. A steady and unwavering course in love, allegiance, or conviction. Firm determination to adhere to a cause or purpose.
