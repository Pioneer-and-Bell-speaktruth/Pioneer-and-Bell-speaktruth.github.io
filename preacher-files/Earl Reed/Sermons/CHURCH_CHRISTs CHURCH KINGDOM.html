IV. DESTINATION OF THE CHURCH

A. TWO Possible

1. Faithful = Heaven

a. Walk in “the light” of Gods word (1 John 1:1-7)

b. Be faithful to Christ to the point of death (Revelation 2:10)

c. Faithful to Heaven (1 Corinthians 15:24-26)

2. Unfaithful = Hell

a. Faithful can fall/leave Christ (1 Corinthians 10:12) b, End of fallen Christians worse than alien sinner (2 Peter 2:20-22)

A. Salvation is ONLY 'in' Christ
