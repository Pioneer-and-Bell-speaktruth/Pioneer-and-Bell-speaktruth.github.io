Word Of Faith Which We Preach

THE WORD OF FAITH WHICH WE PREACH (Romans 10:1-17) “Faith” can be used in different ways. There is “personal” faith: the belief and trust in something or someone. But today we are interested in WHAT IS BELIEVED, the message, the teaching: the faith. Some today say what they preach and teach (their “faith”) comes to them DIRECTLY from God. These modern day prophets so delude the masses that people come to them in droves. To defend their sin, they call all who doubt them “false teachers”, or worse. But we must remember what Jesus said of the deluded religious leaders of HIS day, “…They be blind leaders of the blind. And if the blind lead the blind, both shall fall into the ditch.” (Matthew 15:14) THEIR faith (teaching) comes out of their own heads, in voices they hear (they claim to hear). BUT where does the faith come from that WE PREACH? That will be the objective of this lesson: To show the source of the true Gospel, the Faith, of Christ, so we may KNOW that that is what we preach today. GOD HAS ALREADY REVEALED HIS “FAITH” TO MANKIND through a “chain”! (Don’t need to wait for a “special” leading or calling)
