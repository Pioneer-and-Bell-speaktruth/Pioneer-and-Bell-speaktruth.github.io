WALKING WITH GOD Micah 6:8 (KJV) He hath shewed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God? Enoch walked with God (Genesis 5:24) Noah walked with God (Genesis 6:9) The principles* in this verse have been required in every dispensation/covenant with God. The LORD requires….
