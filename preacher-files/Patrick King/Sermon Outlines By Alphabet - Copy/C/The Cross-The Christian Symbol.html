The History of a Cross in Paganism

Examples: Ankh- Egyptian; symbol of life and fertility, Triskele- Triple goddess & circle of life, Celtic-symbol of faith and protection [St. Patrick’s Day], and Pentacle- five-pointed star; symbol of protection and power, also represents air, water, fire, and earth

“The original form of the cross symbol is believed to have come from the first letter of the name Tammuz, the capital ‘T’23. Tammuz was worshipped as a god of fertility and agriculture, and his connection to growth and rebirth became intertwined with the cross symbol2. The cross symbol was honored as a religious symbol by the people of Assyria and Babylon more than 800 years before the Christian era3. Over time, this symbol underwent transformations and eventually became a significant emblem within early Christian communities2.” -combination of multiple sources
