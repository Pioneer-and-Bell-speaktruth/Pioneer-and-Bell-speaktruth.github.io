Discussion: Part 1 (AM)

  1. Why Does God Allow Tragedies?
    1. James 1:2-4 Temptations are allowed to test or try our faith.
    2. James 1:12-18 True tragedies, temptations, or evil comes from the devil and those that work for him, not from God. God is not to blame, he causes it to rain on the just and the unjust. Tragedies happen whether we are obedient to God or not. Some problems come about in our lives because of our own sin; not diseases and death.
    3. Abraham and the sacrifice of Isaac; God already knew Abraham’s heart and mind, but he gave Abraham the opportunity to prove himself. Isaac could not die or remain dead, because God and already made promises concerning Isaac and God always keeps his Word. Abraham believed in God and his Word and that is why he obeyed.
  2. If God Created the Universe, Then Who Created God?
    1. God always was and always will be. He is an infinite God. (John 12:35, I Pet. 1:23, I John 2:17)
    2. The only reason we think of the idea that someone or something had to of created God is because of what we observe in nature. All things are created or exist because of God. Even the things that man “invents” is due to the fact that God created man. Scientists won’t admit that creation is observable, but that is the real reason why they believe logically that God had to be created, also the fact that false gods were created by the imagination of men.
      1. Neh. 9:6 “Thou, even thou, art LORD alone; thou hast made heaven, the heaven of heavens, with all their host, the earth, and all things that are therein, the seas, and all that is therein, and thou preservest them all; and the host of heaven worshippeth thee.”
      2. Isaiah 45:12 “I have made the earth, and created man upon it: I, even my hands, have stretched out the heavens, and all their host have I commanded.”
    3. Provb. 16:4, 26:10 God created people, but he did not create them evil or wicked. Man makes that decision on his own.
  3. Where is the Proof That God Exists?
    1. The proof of God’s existence is all around us.
      1. Psalm 19:1 “The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork.” Rom. 1:18-24
    2. People choose to ignore the evidence and not believe. (Adam & Eve, Sodom and Gomorrah, the Jews throughout history but also in the first century, many people still today)
      1. Evidence is not a word that most atheist expect to hear from a “religious” person. Apologetics- giving evidence and arguments to prove something.
      2. Isaiah 1:18-20
    3. Just because something can be explained by science doesn’t mean that God doesn’t exist. People focus too much on the word miracles. The creation of everything in the universe, including man, was a miraculous event but that doesn’t mean that the processes left behind are miraculous. Just observe the evidence.
  4. Why Doesn’t God Show Himself?
    1. He does show himself, Psalm 19:1 “The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork.” However, they mean literally God showing himself and not a representation of himself or his work.
    2. Osama B. and Sadam Hussain rarely showed themselves, but we still believed they existed and were alive, why? They left evidence their destruction. God leaves his evidence all around as well so that we don’t have to see him physically to know that he exists. (Scientific Evidence: Wind, stars singing [Job 48:7- 1932 & 1962; March 23, 2015; Dr. John Pasley from the Department of Physics at the University of York; During an experiment a pulse was fired at the star, “generating a series of pressure pulses: a sound wave. “…the sound generated was at such a high frequency that it would have left even bats and dolphins struggling! “…nearly a trillion hertz…– six million times higher than that which can be heard by any mammal! When they are accumulating new material stars could generate sound in a very similar manner to that which we observed in the laboratory – so the stars might be singing – but, since sound cannot propagate [travel] through the vacuum of space, no one can hear them.], the paths of the sea [Psalms 8:8- Matthew Fontaine Maury (1806-1873) discovered the ocean currents and was nicknamed the “Pathfinder of the Seas” and he told his family that he was inspired by Psalms 8:8, the life is in the blood [Lev. 17:11; In 1620, William Harvey an English physician, discovered the full extent of the blood’s circulatory system throughout the body and provided evidence that the body in regards to life was highly depended on the circulation of blood. It wasn’t until later that it was fully recognized by science when Anton Von Leeuwenhoek wrote his papers on the circulation of blood. Leeuwenhoek is famous for inventing the simple light microscope and discovering the cell; he called it that because it resembled the shape of a prison cell.]

Part 1 Conclusion:

  1. Four questions have been answered this morning and hopefully there is enough evidence and scripture here to help you teach anyone, not just atheists, that might ask these questions.
  2. Hebrews 11:1 “Now faith is the evidence of things hoped for, the substance of things not seen.” (KJV) The evidence of God is all around us and in us. The question is what kind of faith do you have? The devils believe and tremble but there won’t be any devils in heaven, why because “by works a man is justified and not by faith only.” James in chapter 2 used Abraham as an example. It was him doing something that demonstrated his faith. Did Abraham earn the salvation of God, no he simply did that which he was commanded to do. He was an unprofitable servant because he did that which was his duty to do. (Luke 17:7-10)
  3. Are we willing to demonstrate our faith and be obedient to God and his commandments?
