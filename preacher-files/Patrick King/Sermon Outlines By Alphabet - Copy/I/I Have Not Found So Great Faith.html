INTRODUCTION: Scripture Reading- Matt. 8:5-13

  1. About the Text: Matthew 8:5-13

1) Every word in the Bible is important.

2) Every word in the Hebrew Old Testament and Greek New Testament is a word chosen by God both as to its form and use.

3) Ephesians 3:3-5

“How that by revelation he made known unto me the mystery; (as I wrote afore in few words, Whereby, when ye read, ye may understand my knowledge in the mystery of Christ) Which in other ages was not made known unto the sons of men, as it is now revealed unto his holy apostles and prophets by the Spirit;”

4) God’s meaning can be seen clearly in Galatians 3:16 “Now to Abraham and his seed were the promises made. He saith not, And to seeds, as of many; but as of one, And to thy seed, which is Christ.”

5) The entire incident of this text is important and significant lessons can be found in each word and verse.

6) The majority of this lesson focuses on verse 10, but then we will look at 11-12 in the last point.
