   C. Discuss the songwriter: Tillit S. Teddlie

         1. Nationality: American; Spouse: Edna Webb; Children: Pete

         2. Tillit Sidney Teddlie:

(June 3, 1885 – Aug. 17, 1987) He was a singing teacher, composer, publisher, and gospel preacher in the Lord’s church. In 1903, he was baptized into Christ, and also taught his first shape note singing school. He composed his first song in 1906. Lifetime: he composed 130 songs, published 14 song books.
